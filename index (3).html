🎜 About this project

SDA Music Realm is building the world’s first comprehensive audio-visual hymn library for the Seventh-day Adventist Church — recording every hymn in every language across Africa and beyond. Our goal is to preserve sacred music heritage, equip congregations with unfamiliar hymns, serve as an evangelistic outreach tool, and bring the joy of worship music to every SDA member regardless of location.
